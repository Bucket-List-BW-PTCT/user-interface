About Us

This project is originally built by the students of lambda school to meet the requirement to be graded for one sprint. The team is composed of six students. Two students of WebPT12 responsible for user interface and four students responsible for the ReactJS Applo application. The links to the ReactJS application are on the navigation bar (Sign In and Sing Up). The present website is built by Jason Sorio and Daniel Kanangila.

BucketList is a free online tool. We also built this for ourselves as a way to keep track of all the amazing things we wanted to do in life.

Project managed by Bryce Evans.

Live is short

Sleep in a five-star hotel in France, swim with dolphins in the Gulf of Mexico, or drink wine in Monaco what’s on your bucket list? For too many people it takes a terrifying illness, hitting retirement age, or some other life altering event to honestly begin thinking about all the things to do before you die. In many of these cases, it will then be too late to turn these dreams into a reality. Life is uncertain, and you will undoubtedly be thrown curve-balls throughout. You cannot be sure that in ten or twenty years you will be physically, emotionally, or financially able to do all of the things that you desire, like climbing Mount Everest in Himalayas or hiking between the cities of Buenos Aires. Don’t wait for that “perfect time” to begin—make your bucket list not because you are dying but because you want to live!
